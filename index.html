Foundational Documents

Website redesign for XYZ Company

The first part of the XYZ Company website redesign project established essential foundational documents. We created a project charter to define the project's vision, goals, stakeholders, and the project manager's authority. A RACI chart clarified team roles and responsibilities, ensuring accountability. A predictive SDLC approach was selected to structure the project efficiently. Finally, a requirements management plan was developed to document, trace, and manage all project requirements, maintaining control over the budget and schedule. These deliverables set the stage for a successful project.

Website redesign for XYZ Company

In this part of the project I successfully created five key deliverables to ensure effective management and execution of the website redesign. First, I determined the project cost and performance using Earned Value Management (EVM) to accurately track the financial and schedule status. Second, I managed change by documenting necessary adjustments and proposing solutions for approval, keeping the project on course. Third, I compiled and presented a comprehensive project status update, detailing costs, timelines, risks, and overall progress to senior managers. Fourth, I created a complete communications management plan to establish clear communication expectations and processes. Finally, I developed a detailed project management plan, consolidating all components and tailoring them to meet organizational needs and priorities. These deliverables provided clear insights and control over the project's progress and ensured its successful completion.
